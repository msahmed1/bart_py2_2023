Your aim in this task is to win points by pumping the balloon up. You may pump the balloon up as many times as you wish but pressing 'Air' button:

  1. Every pump will give you more points
  2. But it also increases the risk of the baloon bursting.
  3. The speed at with you pump does not affect the likelyhood of the baloon bursting.

If you burst the baloon you loose the points from that baloon

You can stop pumping the balloon up at any point and collect the points you have earnt, by pressing the 'Collect Points' button

There are 30 baloons in this block.
